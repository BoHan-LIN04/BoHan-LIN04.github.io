Accepted for publication in IEEE Transactions on Artificial Intelligence (TAI-2026-Mar-A-00547.R1). This work addresses the problem of generator bias in federated learning scenarios characterized by low client participation and model heterogeneity, proposing methods to improve training stability and model performance under these challenging conditions.
